BWXAC - Black and White studio X Academia Cafe

BWXAC "The Artist's Playground" is concerned with Art, Design and Fashion, Literature, Music, Photography, Makeup artistry, Architecture, Intellectual activities, and more.

BWXAC can be defined as a workspace for everyone with or without an art background who have an intense passion for learning art literature and have an admiration for different forms of art. It is about exploring art together and sharing our experiences with other artworks. An appealing way of exploring Literature, Design and Fashion, Music, Intellectual activities, and art and its focus areas.

This is why BWXAC is inclusive - everyone with or without a background in art is welcome.

At BWXAC, we have a diverse selection of books, spanning various genres and subjects. Our collection is meticulously curated to include both popular titles and hidden gems, ensuring there's something for every reader. From fiction to non-fiction, classics to contemporary works, we prioritize inclusivity and aim to represent diverse voices and perspectives.

At BWXAC, we provide a dedicated workspace for artists from different backgrounds, fostering a collaborative and creative environment that nurtures artistic endeavors. Our workspace is designed to inspire and support artists in their diverse artistic practices.

Events

At BWXAC, we host a variety of exciting events that bring our community together and foster a vibrant atmosphere of creativity, learning, and connection. Our events are carefully curated to cater to different interests and passions, offering something for everyone to enjoy.

We regularly host art exhibitions that showcase the works of both emerging and established artists. These exhibitions provide a platform for artists to display their creativity and engage with a wider audience. Visitors can immerse themselves in the world of art, exploring different styles, techniques, and themes.

Art Exhibitions

Our calendar is filled with workshops and classes conducted by experienced artists and professionals. These sessions cover a wide range of artistic disciplines, including painting, drawing, sculpture, photography, and more. Participants can learn new techniques, enhance their skills, and gain valuable insights from experts in their respective fields.

Workshops and Classes

Our open mic nights provide a platform for poets, musicians, spoken word artists, and performers of all kinds to showcase their talent. These lively evenings celebrate artistic expression and provide a supportive space for artists to share their work and connect with a like-minded community.

Open Mic Nights

We encourage collaborative projects that bring artists from different backgrounds together to create something unique. These projects foster artistic growth, cultural exchange, and the opportunity to collaborate with diverse perspectives, resulting in innovative and inspiring works of art.

Collaborative Projects

We believe in giving back to the community through art. We organize community art initiatives such as mural painting, art workshops for children, and art-based fundraisers for social causes. These events aim to make art accessible to everyone and create positive impact through creative expressions.
